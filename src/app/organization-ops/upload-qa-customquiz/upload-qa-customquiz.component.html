Prepare a quiz that best suits your needs
  1. Every Questions CSV you upload must have the following columns:
    • • An "id" column". This is required to give marks to the users for that question
    • • A "Question_text" column. This is the question you want to ask the candidates
    • • A "Domain" column. This places your questions in one of the domains (Ex: 1 - Technology | 2 - Marketing)
  2. Every Answers CSV you upload must have the following columns:
    • • A "Question_related_to" column which will have the "id" value of the Question to which this answer is related to
    • • A "Weightage" column which has the weightage of the Answer
    • • An "Answer_text" column which has the answer choices to be displayed to the candidate
  3. Please download the sample template to get a better idea of how you should upload your CSV files
