About Us

We are a team of engineers who want to solve the employment crisis in India.



Our portal is designed in way to access the skill set and capacity of the Appplicant accurately and recommend jobs to the suitable candidate against a requirement from the industry.


The best way to access an applicant's inclination towards a particular domain is not just by looking at their resume. This is why along with the resume we ask the candidate to answer relevant questions which can be processed to give factual data.


Our assessment consists of three rounds. The first round is a generic round to determine which domain the candidate would excel the most at. The next round has questions on the domain that got selected in the previous round. Here, knowledge of all the sub domains are tested and the top two sub domains are taken into consideration for the third and final round.


Along with the quiz, we also have a chatbot which acts as a Career Counsellor. The applicants who are truly confused about which path they should take can interact with the chatbot and get their queries resolved.
