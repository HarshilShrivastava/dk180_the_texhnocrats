  1. You can attempt this quiz even without signing up on our portal. However for viewing and applying for recommended jobs, you should have an account with us
  2. The quiz consists of 3 rounds as follows:
    • Round 1: Domain level round
      • In this round your knowledge in top level domains will be tested (Domains currently on our portal are technology and marketing). Your best performing domain will be forwarded to round 2 (say Domain X) Also you need to cross a threshold of 70% in a domain in this round for it to be your best performing domain, if not you will be asked to re-attempt round 1
    • Round 2: General sub-domain level round
      • This round will test your knowledge in the sub-domains of Domain X. As of now, we have 4 sub domains for technology and marketing each. The 2 subdomains in which you perform best will be considered for the final round. Again, the threshold of 70% needs to be crossed in each of the top 2 sub-domains. If not, you will be redirected to the 2nd round of the 2nd best domain of round 1 (Eg: If in round 2, you could not clear the threshold for tech sub-domains you will be redirected to round 2 of marketing domain)
    • Round 3: Sub-domain specific round
      • Congrats! You made it to the last round! In this round, we aim to dive deeper into your top 2 subdomains from round 2 and really test your knowledge. The reason we need to be so thorough is because post this round, you will be recommended jobs based on the marks you score here.
Our aim is to target your skillset as accurately as possible so that you can apply for jobs you may genuinely be interested in :)

Hope you find what you’re looking for, best of luck!
