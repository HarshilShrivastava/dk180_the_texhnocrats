Frequently Asked Questions:

Why are all the questions in the quiz not compulsory? Quiz-related help
Since this is the first beta release, we have not kept each question compulsory for testing purposes. However this feature will be added in the upcoming releases.
Why can I not create a candidate account? Account related account_circle
Please make sure you have filled the first time user registration form before trying to create a candidate profile. If you are still facing issues, please let us know by either filling out the feedback form or getting in touch with either of us from the Contact Us page in the footer.
Where do I find the feedback form? Feedback related feedback
The feedback form link is provided in the footer in every page. However, you can also click here for the same.
Can I apply for job without attempting the quiz? Job Application related question_answer
Even though we do provide an option to search for jobs directly to apply, we highly recommned that you take the assessment and apply for the jobs recommended to you because they will be more suited to your capacity and you will be a better fit for those companies.
Where can I view my Job Application? Job Application related question_answer
After applying for the job, you can view your application in the Applied Jobs section.
Where should I upload my resume? Resume related assignment
After sighing up on the portal and making an account with us, you will be directed to a page to give your full name on the application and upload your resume. Please note that we accept resumes only in .pdf format.
How to decide which job to apply for? Job Application related question_answer
You are encouraged to take the assessment and apply for the recommended jobs. However, if you are still unsure about what you want to pursue, our chatbot (present at bottom right of every page) can act as a career counsellor. Please write your queries and get them resolved. If you need further help, do not hesitate to contact us.
Why am I asked irrelevant questions in the quiz? Quiz related help
The first round of the quiz has questions from all domains (mainly technology and marketing). this is a general assessment for all candidates to realize where their true interest lies. The next two rounds will be domain specific only.
Is it necessary to give all the three rounds? Quiz related help
Yes, before moving on to the next round, it is neccesary to have completed the previous round. You need not answer all the questions but we highly recommend that you do because the domains and sub domains will be selected from the results of the previous rounds.
Is there a time limit for attempting the quiz? Quiz related help
Yes, you'll be given a total of 20 minutes to finish all the questions of all the three rounds.
How many questions are there in each round? Quiz related help
There are 20 questions in each round.
Do I need to pay to apply for a job? Job Application related question_answer
No, applying for a job and getting an interview call is absolutely free. if any recruiter is asking you for a payment, please report them to us so that they may be blocked from doing so.
Can I apply for multiple jobs at once? Job Application related question_answer
Yes, you are allowed to apply for more than one job at our portal.
